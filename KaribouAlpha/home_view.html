Cervitt is a product oriented social networking service for modern b2b businesses.

It’s purpose is to securely share product related information in one place, to keep business people informed in real time.. it enables interactive feedback at any stage of the product lifecycle (1-1 or in groups) to enhance customer experience and meet the needs of the market.

Relevant & Real time

Relevant, personalised info in real time: Post & see products recently launched or planned (to clients or prospects)

Showcase products

Gather Feedback

Process manage: Gather feedback / Have your say. Adapt your product prior to launch or during its lifecycle.

Educate
